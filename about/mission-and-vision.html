Mission

SUNA IST is established to provide life changing ICT education and job opportunities for the learners with aim to realise their fullest potentials in the transformation of knowledge in to development using our products and services.

Vision

SUNA IST intends to assume leading role in training for IT users on usage, creativity and innovation for the creation of performance enhancing products and services to uplift the standard of IT business in the country. The institution hopes to apply its "Homegrown" IT products and services to empower government and non-government institutions in real-life problem solving.

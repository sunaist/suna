SUNA Institute of Science and Technology (SIST) is a science and information technology (IT) educational institution that provides quality IT education and consulting.

Since our inception in September 2013, SIST has been committed to providing the highest quality consultancy services and needs-based training for its clients, both locally and internationally. We are driven by our team’s passion for education, training, development and commitment to excellent service. Currently, the staff strength of SIST stands at 9 with a student population of 224 students. Background

Suna Institute of Science and Technology (SIST) was conceived by Mr. Alhagie Jallow in September, 2013 while he was a final year BSc student majoring Information Systems in the School of Information Technology and Communications at The University of The Gambia.

Alhagie has long dreamed of being able to create positive changes and enhanced happy livelihoods for almost every person he meets and interacts with. He is a young, creative, innovative and results-oriented person who recognized what the potentials of technology and a digital economy could offer towards the advancement of humanity in general, but especially in The Gambia. As a native Gambian, he believes charity begins at home. He began to learn about the most pressing social and technological needs of the Gambian populace. Eventually he became motivated to establish an institute that specializes in both ICT trainings and development of ICT products and consultancy services in order to address prevailing IT problems and challenges in The Gambia. By founding SIST, he fulfilled his desire to effectively begin contributing towards national development and for enhanced living for all Gambians.

Our People

We employ a core team of exceptional individual professionals and draw on a wide array of professional associates and support personnel on an as-needed basis. The focus of our team is on IT consultancy services, project management, materials design and customization, facilitation and ultimately ensuring that the client gets the best possible service. We also draw on a wide array of professional associates, active across the full range of business, civil society, community and government sectors.

SIST has a diverse human resource capacity that consists of individuals that have worked on international, national and local levels with proven track records of delivery in the quality services, education and training programs to relevant organizations, state, private and non-governmental sectors. Within our pool of professionals are people that have worked extensively in different institutions both within and outside the Gambia. It should be noted that our staff and Associates come from a host of backgrounds including national and local government, business and civil society organizations.

Our Guiding Principles

We pursue high quality in all our consultancy services and training intervention and programmes by ensuring quality within the framework of our outlined guiding principles. Our Staff are experts in their field and experienced IT professionals in addition to or visionary approaches to teaching and learning.

  • Fairness and Accuracy
  • Diversity and inclusiveness
  • Innovativeness and creativity
  • Timeliness and convenience
  • Customer or client focus

Our Unique Approaches

  • Hybrid approach (on the ground or online)
  • We provide off- and on-site training
  • Customize course to suit client’s specific needs
  • Course structures are flexible and designed to match clients’ time and budget
  • Client focus and satisfaction

Our Social Responsibility

As part of our commitment to social development, SIST has initiated its ‘Summer Code for Young Programmers’ Project, which aims to build computer coding and programming skills of emergent developers from disadvantaged communities. Kids less than 12 years old formed the cornerstone of our interventions in this regard.

Peace, Welcome to our Department of ICT.

The information explosion together with developments in technology have led to the convergence of traditional communication media and turned the Information Era into a web of integrated communication connections. I am extremely proud of the professionally driven and academic quality of our programs and our ability to address the needs of today’s contemporary Information Technology issues and challenges through our valued and che rished students. We provide Information Communication Technology training programmes for individuals, corporates, government and communities. SIST offers programmes that are fully registered and accredited by the National Training Authority of The Gambia.

Employers are looking for Information Communications & Technology graduates who have a strategic understanding and practical pre-requisite knowledge and skills to curtail the emerging challenges, how they fit together and utilize technology to promote organizational objectives. Additionally, the society and employers seek individuals who have the ability to effectively solve problems and be innovative, possess good practical, technical and communication skills that are ethically inclined and embrace diversity. All of our programs within the Institute address these concepts.

Each of the programs offered in the Department of ICT are designed to provide our students with both broad-based and specialized ICT trainings that emphasizes the application of knowledge, concepts and skills to Information Technology practice and builds conceptual skills and core values needed to become effective and efficient professionals in today’s rapidly changing global environment. In fact, that is the ultimate mission of the ICT Department! You can become part of this highly competitive and futuristic environment by enrolling for one of our exciting programs. Become an information technology specialist, systems administrator, web and database developer or PC repairer and maintenance.

Our programs are designed to meet the specific needs of the working class professionals as well as those who have limited or no work experience. Our course structure is flexible and designed to match clients’ time and budget. Students can take courses online, on the ground, or hybrid (blended approach). They can also take classes on Saturdays or on weekdays including evenings throughout the year. We also provide off- and on-site training.

Suna’s legacy of close relationships and personalized professional trainings is the foundation on which we continue to build. Such personalization - between instructors and students and among the students themselves - encapsulates the difference between us and other professional training institutions. When you enroll in any of our programs, you are embraced by a professional environment made up of a diverse community of seasoned instructors and learners.

The professional and academic quality of our programs, the convenience of our schedules, and more importantly the commitment of our instructors and staff make our programs the most popular in the region and The Gambia by extension.

I hope that you seriously consider the opportunity of getting your certificates from Suna Institute of Science & Technology. It is an investment that will reward you for a lifetime.

Come join this exciting new world of Information Communication Technology in all its facets.

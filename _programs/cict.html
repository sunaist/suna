{{ page.program-name }}

This course is a comprehensive preparatory Information and Communication Technology (ICT) course that serves as a feeder to any other higher level courses within the discipline of Information Technology and Communication. The course will dealt with fundamentals topics in Information Communication Technology such as; Introduction to computers, history of computers, hardware and software, the different kind of hardware and software, operating systems, categories of hardware and software, Graphical user interface, cloud computing, desktop environment and finally to open a Gmail account as the hands-on for students.

The course does not only teach the students the fundamental concepts of ICT but also provide room for applications of every concept learned in class with hands-on. Finally, the Microsoft Office packaged in covered in details ranging from MS Word, MS Excel, MS PowerPoint and MS Access. In each of the above applications, students are given projects to accomplish which are real life challenges within the society.

Among the projects the students will be doing will be to automate any report card of a school which will be a project for excel after the completion of the module and the same applies to all the remaining application. The course will entail, several assignments from all the modules covered in class and there will be group work and presentations as well.

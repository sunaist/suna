Get to know Suna Institute of Science and Technology

SUNA Institute of Science and Technology (SIST) is a science and information technology (IT) educational institution that provides quality IT education and consulting.

Suna Institute of Science and Technology (SIST) was conceived by Mr. Alhagie Jallow in September, 2013 while he was a final year BSc student majoring Information Systems in the School of Information Technology and Communications at The University of The Gambia.

Alhagie has long dreamed of being able to create positive changes and enhanced happy livelihoods for almost every person he meets and interacts with. He is a young, creative, innovative and results-oriented person who recognized what the potentials of technology and a digital economy could offer towards the advancement of humanity in general, but especially in The Gambia.

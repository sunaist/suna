At Suna Institute of Science and Technology (SIST) we will equip you with the necessary skills to succeed as we lead you through the right path. We will walk with you all the way to the end of your goal, which shall ultimately be a successful career in the IT field. We put our students interests first, and our goal is for our students to graduate from SIST with upbeat, enthusiastic and zealous attitudes. With SIST you will have no regrets about your future career goals.

About 90% of our lecturers are young university graduates, a record that is unrivaled by other training institutions in this country. We will continue to add the best university graduates to our teaching faculty, and in the long run 100% will have achieved their degrees within the IT field.

Comparatively, looking at the quality of our teaching faculty, our tuition fees are affordable. We also offer flexible payment plans for those students who may need more time. Here at SIST we feel that affordability in higher education is key to improved national development. The mission of SIST is to be an institution for all those who meet our entry requirements and are motivated to acquire the best quality IT educations.

SUNA Institute of Science and Technology, also offer remedial classes for those currently in the senior school system and all school leavers who already sat to the West African Senior Secondary Certificate Examination (WASSCE) wishing to improve their grades in the some subjects.

Highly qualified and experienced teachers with proper understanding of the senior school syllabi / syllabus handle this programme. Not only are they capable in delivering on subject matters, our teachers are prepared to train students on examination techniques.

With the help of SUNA IST “private WASSCE classes” Most of our alumni students are in the wind with excellence pursuing their career in different tertiary institute.
